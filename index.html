Color Technology

85% of visible color — not just 52%

Standard CMYK printing reproduces about half of what the human eye sees. Our Heidelberg XL75 8-color ECG (Extended Color Gamut) press adds orange, green, and violet ink stations — hitting colors in packaging that standard presses simply cannot match.

ECG — 8 colors (CMYK + OGV) 85%
Standard CMYK — 4 colors 52%
Brilliant oranges, grass greens, deep violets — in a single pass
No spot color plates needed — fewer setups, lower cost at scale
G7 certified color control — consistent run-to-run
Pairs with matte, soft-touch, gloss, and spot-UV finishes
